Let's split the bill!

This application is designed to help split the bill among your group of friends. This process will take you on the steps to split your bill evenly, including added tips and taxes.

Let's have some fun!

If you're feeling lucky, test your luck and have one person pay for the whole bill. Just like the famous game of the same name, one unlucky person in your group will be chosen to pay for the bill. Click on the button to find out who is the unlucky one!
